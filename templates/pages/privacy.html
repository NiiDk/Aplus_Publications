Institutional Data Protection Standards

1. Data Collection

APLUS PUBLICATIONS collects essential academic metadata (Names, Institution, Academic Role) solely to provide tailored educational resources and professional services to students, teachers, and schools.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used to process textbook acquisitions, manage institutional quote requests, and notify users of critical curriculum updates or new publication releases.

3. Data Security

We implement professional-grade security protocols to protect academic and financial data. We do not sell or share user data with third-party marketing entities.

4. Academic Records

Order history and resource download logs are maintained to help schools and individuals track their academic progression and resource utilization over time.
